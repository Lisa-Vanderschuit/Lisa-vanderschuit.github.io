My Story

From the time I was really young, I have always been fascinated in the design of things whether it be for practical or aesthetic purposes. Harold from Harold and the Purple Crayon has been one of my biggest inspirations when it comes to design. Like Harold, designers also help to shape the environment around them. The lesson that I learned from Harold was that we are only limited by our own imagination!

About Me

2014, I finished my last year at Algonquin, graduating with an Advanced Diploma with Honours (Dean’s List) in Graphic Design, a 3-year program. I'm currently @Shopify where I get to work with the best team ever.

What have I been up too?

Recently, I've been putting together a blog! I'm not quite sure what direction it will go in except that it will be related to my experience at Shopify. More details to come.

Last year, I tried downhill skiing for the first time ever! I started off on the bunny hill, then by the end of the week I even tried a black diamond route, by accident.

Spring 2014, I was finishing up my graphic design program at Algonquin. For the first part of my last semester, I had the great opportunity to work on and help organize the Level 14 grad website. My role was to collect, program, and design the section where the student work was to be displayed.

For the last six weeks of my program, I had my coop placement at Uniforge, also home to the Games For All lounge, where I was able to work with some really great people. I learned a lot and was given great tips on how to improve my design and coding skills.
